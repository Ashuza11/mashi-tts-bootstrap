The first synthetic voice in Mashi

Mashi is spoken by about 3 million people and had no speech technology of any kind. There was no dataset, no ASR, and no TTS. We built the first Mashi speech dataset with 3 speakers, 5 domains, and 723 curated clips. We fine tuned XTTS v2 on 67 minutes of audio in one training run. Every clip below is machine generated Mashi. Press play.

All clips 2.0 to 11.3 s, mono 22,050 Hz, cut only at natural pauses; transcripts verified by a native speaker. The mrl domain is assembled from 95 unique component recordings of murhula.com's talking clock (with the owner's permission), so its minutes overstate its acoustic diversity. This is disclosed by design.
